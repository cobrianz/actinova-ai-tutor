## ğŸ¯ Creating Your First Presentation

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Enter Topic**
   - Type what you want to present about
   - Example: "History of Artificial Intelligence"
   - Supports up to 500 characters

2. **Choose Style**
   - Professional (corporate, clean)
   - Creative (colorful, engaging)
   - Minimal (simple, focused)

3. **Select Slides**
   - Use slider to choose between 5-50 slides
   - More slides = more detailed content
   - Recommended: 10-20 for balanced presentations

4. **Set Difficulty**
   - **Beginner**: Free for all users
   - **Intermediate**: Pro required (in-depth content)
   - **Advanced**: Pro required (expert-level content)

5. **Generate**
   - Click "Generate Presentation" button
   - Wait for AI to create content (usually 10-30 seconds)
   - You'll see a loading animation
   - Automatic redirect to library when complete
